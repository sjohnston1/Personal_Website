Spencer Johnston is currently a Senior at Loyola Unviersity of Chicago. He is studying Information Technology with a minor in Management.

Spencer started his journey in Seoul, South Korea for the first eight months of his life. After being adopted, he moved to Minneapolis, Minnesota. From there, he would move around the world, touching down in Lyon, France; Haddington, Scotland; Atlanta, Georgia; Lexington, Kentucky; Portland, Oregon; and Chicago, Illinois where he currently resides.

In Portland, he began his high school education at Jesuit Portland before transferring to Loyola Academy in Wilmette, Illinois. It was at Loyola Acadmey that Spencer fell in love with coding and took his schools offered Honors Computer Science course opting into taking the AP exam at the end of the school year. He would then accepted into Loyola Unviersity of Chicago where he would pursue Information Technoloy.

While at Loyola University, he has taken classes covering a wide range of topics, from World History to Accounting. Yet it was his classes in Computer Science that kept his interest taking courses involving databases, website design and creation, and object oriented programming. It ended up being databases that fascinated Spencer the most though, taking Business Information Systems, Database Management, Database Administration and Datamining & Warehousing. Not only did he apply his knowledge of databases within those specific courses, but he applied it outside of them. An example being implementing MongoDB into a wesbite for his Clientside Web Development course.

Spencer enjoys golfing with friends, spending time with family, and playing videogames. He has been forturnate to have started golf at a young age and managed to play a couple years in high school. Even though he does not play college golf, he managed to teach junior golf through his first three years of college to young children starting out. Spencer's nuclear and extended family all live within fifteen minutes of each other so spending time with them has become a key facet in his life. Finally, he has been a huge gamer since he has been young. His love for videogames has culminated in him taking a video game design course during his time at Loyola to learn about the development that went into creating some of his favorites.

Outside of his hobbies, Spencer also has worked numerous jobs. Within the field of golf, he has worked both as a junior golf instructor and a caddie at Biltmore Country Club in North Barrington, Illinois. He was a junior golf instructor there for three years and is still a caddie there coming upon his seventh year there. He worked as a Coding Intern for three months at CarAdvise based out of Barrington, Illinois using HTML, CSS, JS and Java to create interfaces and fix bugs throughout their web interface and iOS application. He has also been working at Loyola University of Chicago as a Undergraduate Research Assistant since August of 2018. During the same semester, he also was a student instructor for the Introduction to Object Oriented Programming course.
